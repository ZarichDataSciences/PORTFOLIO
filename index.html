A brief history of IT time and space for me begins in 1999 when I began an internship in PC support. In time I became a server and network engineer. This lasted for seven years when a radical and new calling began; I trained to become a line flight attendant. What a wonderful and fanciful ride the past fifteen years have been. But, like for most, the COVID-19 pandemic ground my industry to a halt, and I found myself not only desiring my IT career again, but looking for new educational opportunites to tune up my skills and position me for success.

To accomplish this I enrolled at ElevenFiftyAcademy in their software development program. At the same time I enrolled in Ivy Tech's network security program. Currently I'm studying and working with C# .NET and Cisco Systems to create a unique and powerful IT combination of skills. My goal is to pursue my CompTIA Network+, Security+ and Cisco CCNA as well as continue my studies in C# and .NET ecosystem. I also study and enjoy Linux and backend server infrastructures.
